# Getting Started with Age Calculator

Welcome to Age Calculator! This guide will help you get started.

## What is Age Calculator?

Calculate exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes from birth date to any date.

## How to Use

1. Open the tool on the home page
2. Enter your data
3. Click the button to process
4. View and save your results

## Tips

- All data is processed locally in your browser
- Your data is automatically saved
- Works on all devices
